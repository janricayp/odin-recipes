Lasagna Recipe

Making this lasagna recipe a day ahead and refrigerating overnight allows the spices to meld and gives it exceptional flavor.

Ingredient

  1. 1 ½ pounds lean ground beef
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  5. 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  6. 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  7. 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  8. 2 ½ teaspoons salt, divided
  9. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  10. 12 dry lasagna noodles
  11. 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  12. ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  13. 2 large eggs, beaten
  14. 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  15. 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
  16. 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
